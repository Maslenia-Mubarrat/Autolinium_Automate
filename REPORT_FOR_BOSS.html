Project Autolinium Update

Accelerated Sprint Progress
Report Date: March 10, 2026

To the Management Team,

We are pleased to report that the development pace has massively accelerated tonight. By strictly adhering to the newly approved "10-Day Accelerated Master Plan," we have successfully finalized the deliverables for both Day 1 and Day 2 in a single, continuous sprint.

The system is now actively enforcing accountability through digital tracking.

1. Global Meeting Oversight (Day 1 Complete)

The system now features a dedicated, secure Hub for managing and auditing all office meetings.

  • Executive Surveillance: Management now has a "God-View" Master List that tracks every internal and client meeting.
  • Attendance Verification: The database now digitally logs the exact moment an employee confirms they have joined a meeting, eliminating manual roll-calls.

2. Task Engine & Accountability Enforcement (Day 2 Complete)

We have completely overhauled how tasks are assigned and tracked to prevent delays and missed deadlines.

  • Targeted Assignment: Management can now dispatch tasks instantly to specific employees via a direct dropdown interface in the Command Center.
  • The "Late" Protocol: The system now automatically flags overdue tasks in RED across the entire office loop.
  • Mandatory Explanations: The software has been weaponized against avoidance. If an employee is late on a task, the system physically blocks them from marking it as completed until they type a valid, mandatory explanation for the delay.

Strategic Outlook

With the tracking foundation locked in, tomorrow we transition into Day 3: The Feedback Loop. We will be building the "Review Engine," allowing Management to evaluate and rate these completed tasks. This sets the stage for connecting the scores directly to the KPI performance metrics later this week.
